Efficient Functionality: How CitizenWatch Works

1

Image Upload and Description

Users can easily capture and upload images of maintenance or sanitation issues encountered in their communities directly through the app. Along with the image, users can provide a description detailing the nature and severity of the issue, providing crucial context for local authorities.

2

Geotagging and Location Identification

The app automatically geotags each uploaded image, pinpointing the exact location of the reported issue on a map interface.This geotagging feature ensures precise identification of problem areas, facilitating swift action by local authorities.

3

Notification to Local Authorities

Once an image is uploaded, CitizenWatch sends instant notifications to the relevant local authorities or government agencies responsible for addressing the reported issue.Authorities receive real-time alerts, enabling them to promptly review and respond to citizen complaints.

4

Status Tracking and Updates

Users can track the status of their reported issues within the app, receiving updates on the progress of resolution efforts.From initial review to final resolution, users remain informed about the status of their complaints, fostering transparency and accountability.
